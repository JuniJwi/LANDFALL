Members have a limit of 3 active character slots.

  • There is a 3 month cooldown to character creation.
  • The cooldown can be waived by spending activity points.
  • Members can retire a character to free up a slot. There is no limit to the number of retired characters a member may have.

To retire a character, submit a retirement request [here].

  • Retiring a character is free.
  • Retirement is diegetic within our plot. While you can retire freely, you need to coordinate it with us first.
  • Retired characters will either be permanently killed off or fundamentally changed.
  • If the latter, you are permitted to unretire them after a while if you want to have fun with their new changed form.
  • You cannot un-retire an OC if you are using up all of your active character slots.
  • The cost of ‘un-retirement’ is the same as character creation for activating your character slot– either you need to wait for your 3 month cooldown to be up, or spend activity points to waive the cooldown.
  • Yes… you can technically re-retire and re-un-retire and re-re-retire (and etcetera) your character. But if we notice a pattern of abuse, we may revoke your retirement rights.
